section: Before You Start
---

1. In the **Getting Started** page, you have to click the **Register** in **WhatsApp Business**
2. You will be directed to [https://business.kata.ai](https://business.kata.ai) and have to follow all the steps for registering your WhatsApp Business number. Learn more about [registering your WhatsApp number](/business-dashboard/how-to/get-started)
3. After successfully registered your WhatsApp Business, you can go back to Kata Omnichat to connect WABA as your channel in the dashboard
